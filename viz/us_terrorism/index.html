Story:
Terrorist attack on the US, when, where, and why?
The USA is always one of the biggest targets for terrorist attack. When and where did these attacks take place? Is there any relationship among the type of attack, the scale of the attack, and geolocation? Does attack always happen in the city?
From the following visualizations, we may sense some of the hidden relationships to help understanding why attack happen.
1. In contrast to world trend, amount of attacks in the US is decreasing.
2. Attack usually will not take place in November.
3. Most of the attacks are related to firearms and bombs.
4. Attack usually happens in the place with a large population.
5. The crime rate is not related to attack.
